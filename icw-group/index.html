How I Build a Data Product

A repeatable workflow I ran for Intuit's 6 concurrent data products — adapted from the same discipline I applied to FDA-regulated SaMD releases at Dexcom and ITIL-governed enterprise releases at ServiceNow.

  1. 1Discover — meet with vertical analytic stakeholders; align on business goals & questions to answer.
  2. 2Define metrics — keyed to specific business objectives and product-user behaviors.
  3. 3Spec triggers — events, source systems, third-party data needs.
  4. 4Ingest — pipelines for first-party + third-party data, with quality gates.
  5. 5Transform — SQL transformations to modeled tables / marts.
  6. 6Deliver — consumption layer for dashboards, AI-driven insights, end-user decisioning.
  7. 7Operate — hold pipelines to defined SLAs; partner on backfills, schema changes, and incident response.
